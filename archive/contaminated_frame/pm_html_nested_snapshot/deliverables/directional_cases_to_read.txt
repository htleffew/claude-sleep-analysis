=== CASE 10 (post_idx=2347, sel=directional_temporal, sub=ClaudeCode, nudge=1, n_quotes=1) ===
Tip for saving tokens on long conversations, direct from the CC leak Couldn't help but dig into the leaked claude code repo, here's a good finding for saving tokens:

Naturally, CC caches your sessions to save on token costs. However, this cache only lasts 60 minutes

So if you're in the middle of a long task, and you have to step aside for a break, you should ping it before 60 minutes run out, otherwise you will end up burning the **entire conversation's worth of tokens** on your next prompt

It can be a simple "hi" on haiku, nothing big

The real pro tip is using something like `/loop` if you have to step away for a few hours. For example, `/loop say "hi" and nothing else to me every 55 minutes for the next 7 hours"` - if, for instance, you wanted to sleep or something ridiculous like that before getting back to your claude session

This screenshot, btw, is directly from `/services/api/claude.ts` - a whopping 3,420 line file (that's gotta say something about the quality of claude building itself, as anthropic loves to boast about)
