Largest PMI / P(nudge|lexicon) tells you which user-disclosed context most strongly predicts a Claude sleep-nudge.

  - If 'session' wins: model is responding to length cues (LCR Zombie hypothesis).

Directional table tells you whether the disclosure precedes the nudge in the visible exchange (stronger evidence than co-occurrence alone).
