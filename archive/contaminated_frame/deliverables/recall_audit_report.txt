Interpretation
--------------------------------------------------
The recall lower bound is conservative because:
 (a) the structural cue heuristic does not separate Claude-attributed
     nudge phrases from user-voice nudge phrases, so the denominator
     contains some posts where no model utterance exists;
 (b) extractor V2 itself uses several of these structural cues, so
     the test is partly internal-consistency rather than fully
     independent recall measurement.

A formal recall point estimate requires human-labeled ground truth
on a stratified sample and is the principal outstanding methodological
item flagged in the paper.
